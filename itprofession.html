My Profession

1. What is an IT profession for you?

The IT (Information Technology) profession encompasses various roles and job positions related to the management, development, and maintenance of computer systems, software, networks, and data. IT professionals work with technology to support businesses, organizations, and individuals in solving technical challenges and leveraging technology effectively.

2. Why do you think that your course is one of the most high-demand in the society?

For me this course is one of the most high-demand because most aspect in our life they use technology like from businesses, healthcare, education and other facilities and organization that use technology. Also, this field is characterized by constant innovation, and evolving frameworks that requires in a professional setup to effectively implement and leverage emerging tools and technologies.

3. Why do you choose BSIT course?

I decided to enroll in a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program because it will allow me to pursue a career in the IT sector, develop adaptable skills, and advance the development of information-driven solution.

4. What will you become after finishing your course?

After completing my study, I want to do well in the field of information technology and become the best version of myself. I am aware of the significance of being current and adaptable as technology continues to advance quickly. I therefore intend to keep developing my knowledge and abilities by actively learning new programming languages and keeping up with cutting-edge technological developments. In order to effectively contribute to the creation of fresh ideas and the effective implementation of technology-driven projects, I seek to maintain an attitude of lifelong learning. By doing this, I hope to stay at the forefront of industry innovations and trends. I want to have a significant impact on the IT business and help to shape the direction of technology by constantly working to enhance myself.

5. Describe a good IT professional.

IT professionals handle sensitive data, information security, and intellectual property. A good IT professional upholds ethical standards, respects privacy and confidentiality, and follows industry best practices. They demonstrate integrity in their work, maintain professionalism, and prioritize the security and privacy of systems and data.
